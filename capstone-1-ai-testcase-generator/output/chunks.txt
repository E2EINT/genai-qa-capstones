1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 Purpose
This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) document describes the functional and non-functional requirements for the
ShopEasy E-commerce Platform. The platform is designed to provide customers with a seamless online shopping experience
while enabling merchants to manage their products, inventory ,
